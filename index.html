Privacy Policy

On-device learning. No tracking. No data collection.

TL;DR: We don't collect any personal data. MemorizeAnything works entirely on your device.

Overview

MemorizeAnything is an iOS flashcard study app by TapShiftDev that helps you master any subject using spaced repetition and gamification. This policy explains how your information is handled.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Study content or flashcards
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing or app activity

We store locally on your device:

  • Your flashcards and decks
  • Study progress and statistics
  • Achievements and XP levels
  • App preferences and settings

How It Works

  • All flashcards are created and stored only on your device
  • Study sessions and progress tracking happen locally using SwiftData
  • Spaced repetition algorithms run on your device
  • No data is ever transmitted to our servers
  • No internet connection required for core functionality

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit (Optional)

If you purchase premium features:

  • Purchases are processed by Apple's secure payment system
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Apple's privacy policy governs payment data

iCloud (Optional)

If you enable iCloud backup:

  • Your study data syncs via Apple's iCloud
  • Managed entirely by Apple, not us
  • Subject to Apple's iCloud privacy policy

Data Storage

  • All data remains local to your device via SwiftData
  • Settings stored in UserDefaults (on-device only)
  • No cloud storage or remote databases
  • Your device passcode/biometrics protect your data
  • Deleting the app permanently removes all data

Security

  • Data protected by iOS device encryption
  • Face ID / Touch ID authentication (device-level)
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • Regular app updates for security improvements

Children's Privacy

MemorizeAnything is safe for all ages. Since we collect no data, there are no special privacy concerns for children beyond standard device security practices.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual cards, decks, or all data from the app
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device; you control it
  • No tracking: We don't track, profile, or analyze your behavior

Legal Compliance

This policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to process, store externally, or delete upon request.

Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect app changes. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
