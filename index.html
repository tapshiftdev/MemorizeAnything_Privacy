Our Privacy Promise

We don't collect any of your data. Period.

All your flashcards, study progress, and achievements stay on your device. We have no access to your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

None.

MemorizeAnything does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or study data to our servers or any third parties.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data created in MemorizeAnything is stored locally on your device using:

  • SwiftData: Apple's on-device database framework
  • UserDefaults: For app preferences and settings

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up via iCloud (which is managed by Apple, not us).

3. Third-Party Services

MemorizeAnything does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases (Optional)

If you choose to purchase the premium version:

  • Purchases are processed securely by Apple's StoreKit
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • We recommend enabling device encryption and regular backups
  • If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted

5. Children's Privacy

MemorizeAnything is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no special considerations for children's privacy beyond standard device security.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual flashcards, decks, or all data from within the app
  • Export: [If applicable] Export your data in standard formats
  • Portability: Your data stays with you on your device

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data collection practices (which we don't plan to), we will:

  • Update this privacy policy
  • Notify users through the app
  • Require explicit consent for any data collection
